About Me

Hi, I'm Jackie, a web developer/designer and graphic artist with a passion for helping people...and I'm ridiculously detail-oriented. I spent the early part of my career working mainly in the realm of product packaging design before moving into a role as a marketing specialist. But, after a while, I just wasn't feeling my corporate career anymore and my heart called me in a different direction.

I wanted to save the world...that's an attainable goal, right? So, I went to nursing school and spent 10+ years working as an oncology nurse alongside some of the most amazing doctors, coworkers, and patients. I learned so much and am beyond grateful for my time as an RN becasue it helped to shape the person that I am today.

I missed being a designer and the creative problem-solving that came with it. I made the decison to transition back into a design-based career, and being the perpetual learner that I am, I focused on increasing my technical skills and abilities in web development. This is a journey that has quite literally come full circle for me and I'm excited to be back to doing what I love.

My Skills
